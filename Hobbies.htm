Trap and Skeet Shooting

I am a National Championship, All-State Team Captain for Massachusetts in shotgun sports. I am ranked 96 in the nation for all SCTP (Scholastic Clay Target Program) competitors. I have captained an All-State team for Team Massachusetts for two years. Also, I am a team "captain" for my local youth shooting team. I have been competing in this sport since the 8th grade and have continued my shooting career into my university life at the University of Maine shooting for the Maine Trap and Skeet Club.
